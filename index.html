EcoHealth is a health tracker application designed to help users monitor their daily health activities and maintain a healthy lifestyle.

About The Project

The idea for EcoHealth stemmed from the need to consolidate health-related information and provide users with a comprehensive tool to track their progress and achieve their health goals.

Features

Daily Activity Tracking: Track your steps, calories burned, and distance traveled each day.Meal Logging: Log your meals and track your calorie intake to maintain a balanced diet.Sleep Monitoring: Monitor your sleep patterns and receive insights to improve your sleep quality.Custom Goals: Set personalized health goals and receive notifications to stay on track.Community Support: Connect with other users, share your progress, and motivate each other on your health journey.

Technologies

React Native: For building cross-platform mobile applications.Redux: For managing application state and data flow.Firebase: For user authentication, data storage, and cloud functions.Material-UI: For designing responsive and accessible user interfaces.

EcoGarden is a gardening application designed to assist gardening enthusiasts in planning, managing, and maintaining their gardens.

About The Project

The inspiration for EcoGarden arose from the growing popularity of gardening and the desire to provide users with a digital tool to simplify the gardening process and enhance their gardening experience.

Features

Garden Planning: Design and plan your garden layout with customizable plots and plant varieties.Plant Library: Access a comprehensive library of plant species with detailed information on care and cultivation.Task Management: Organize and schedule gardening tasks such as watering, fertilizing, and pruning.Harvest Tracking: Track the growth and harvest of your plants to monitor their progress over time.Community Sharing: Connect with other gardeners, share tips and advice, and showcase your garden achievements.

Technologies

React: For building interactive and responsive user interfaces.Node.js: For backend development and server-side logic.MongoDB: For storing garden data and user information.Express.js: For building RESTful APIs and server-side routing.

EcoShop is a sustainable marketplace offering a curated selection of eco-friendly products from environmentally-conscious brands.

About The Project

EcoShop was born out of a shared commitment to promote sustainability and provide consumers with access to ethically sourced and environmentally friendly products.

Features

Product Categories: Browse a wide range of eco-friendly products categorized by type and brand.Product Details: View detailed product information, including materials, manufacturing process, and sustainability certifications.Wishlist: Save your favorite products to your wishlist for easy access and future purchase.Secure Checkout: Enjoy a seamless and secure checkout process with multiple payment options.Community Engagement: Engage with like-minded individuals, share sustainable living tips, and participate in eco-conscious initiatives.

Technologies

React: For building dynamic and interactive user interfaces.Redux: For managing application state and data flow.Node.js: For backend development and server-side logic.MongoDB: For storing product data and user information.Express.js: For building RESTful APIs and server-side routing.
